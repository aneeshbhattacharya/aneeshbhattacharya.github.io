About

Hi! I am currently an senior at IIIT Naya Raipur, India.
My research interests are in domains of Multimodal Learning and HCI & Cognitive Modeling - 3D human pose and facial expression synthesis (AR/VR) and interpreting human gaze data for exploring cognitive features. As a research intern under the guidance of Dr. Aniket Bera at Purdue University, I am currently working on generating genre-agnostic 3D dance pose sequences for music tracks using multimodal data with GANs and attention models.
I have previously worked as a research intern under the guidance of Dr. Jayanta Mukhopadhyay at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur in the domain of HCI and Computer Vision, specifically in the understanding of human cognitive processes using egocentric videos and their corresponding eye-tracker gaze data.
